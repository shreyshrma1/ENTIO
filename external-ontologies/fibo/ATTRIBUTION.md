# FIBO And OMG Commons Attribution

Entio bundles a read-only, reproducible snapshot of the Financial Industry
Business Ontology (FIBO) maintained by the EDM Association and EDM Council.

- Source: https://github.com/edmcouncil/fibo
- Release: `master_2026Q2`
- Commit: `f59157fe156e3d91b1c045222d0a7dc06b7d78a2`
- FIBO license: MIT, as carried by the pinned FIBO repository.
- OMG Commons version: `1.3`
- OMG Commons source: https://www.omg.org/spec/Commons/
- OMG Commons license: MIT, with notices preserved in the bundled ontology
  documents and `LICENSE-OMG-COMMONS-MIT.txt`.

This package is provided for ontology browsing and controlled reuse inside
Entio. Bundling these assets does not imply endorsement by the EDM Association,
EDM Council, Object Management Group, or any contributor. The bundled files are
read-only Entio inputs and are not modified by the application.
